Astro is a modern static site builder that focuses on shipping less JavaScript to the browser.

## Why use Astro?
- **Fast**: minimal client-side JS
- **Flexible**: works with React, Vue, Svelte, and Markdown
- **Fun**: developer-friendly with simple setup
