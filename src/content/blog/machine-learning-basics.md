Machine Learning (ML) is a branch of Artificial Intelligence that enables computers to learn patterns from data and make predictions without explicit programming.

## 10 Essential Topics to Learn
1. **Linear Algebra** — Vectors, matrices, dot products, eigenvalues.
2. **Probability & Statistics** — Distributions, Bayes’ theorem, hypothesis testing.
3. **Data Preprocessing** — Cleaning, normalization, handling missing data.
4. **Supervised Learning** — Regression, classification, common algorithms.
5. **Unsupervised Learning** — Clustering, dimensionality reduction.
6. **Neural Networks & Deep Learning** — Layers, activation functions, backpropagation.
7. **Model Evaluation** — Cross-validation, metrics (accuracy, precision, recall, F1).
8. **Regularization & Optimization** — L1/L2 regularization, gradient descent.
9. **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** — Tokenization, embeddings, transformers.
10. **ML Tools & Frameworks** — scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch.

Mastering these areas gives you the foundation to tackle real-world ML projects.
